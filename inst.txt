
Instructions:

        Navigate Pac-Man through the maze, eating all the dots ('.') to advance to the next level! :)

   1.Use W, S, A, D to move Up, Down, Left, and Right respectively. :)
   2.Gain points by eating dots ('.'). Extra points for completing levels! :)
   3.Avoid the ghosts ,they will eat you! :'(
   4.For pause press P
   5.For resume press R
   6.Press 3 to quit the game.
Bonus Tips:

   Be quick and smart to eat all the dots and avoid the ghosts. :)
   Look out for surprises that might help you! ;)
Press Q to exit these instructions and start your game. Good luck! :)
